    Circular Economy

    The cutting stage of textile manufacturing creates the largest amount of pre-consumer fabric wastage. Statistics show that the cutting process counts for 10%-30% of the waste from the total material intake within fields of fashion, home textiles, furniture.

  • •

    Concept

    Traditionally, cutting waste from production was down-cycled into inferior quality material that would then be used as underlay and backing materials. Instead of down-cycling, why not go up-cycling and produce first-quality furnishing product from recycled textile waste?

  • •

    Etterlivet

    Etterlivet is such a concept which is about to investigate the possibility of conducting low-carbon waste management activities where scrap textile from the industry waste can be up-cycled while maintaining aesthetic quality.
  • •

    Process

    The process involves collecting, sorting and repurposing, a small yet important step towards the large sphere of circular economy.
